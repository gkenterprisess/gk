G.K. Enterprises

Welcome to G.K Enterprises, your premier destination for high-quality cycle parts proudly manufactured and supplied across India. With a legacy dating back to 1998, we have been at the forefront of the industry, consistently delivering excellence in every product. Headquartered in Ludhiana and under the visionary leadership of Tajinderpal Singh, G.K Enterprises has established itself as a trusted name in the market.


At G.K Enterprises, we specialize in crafting a wide range of cycle parts, including Koli Washers, Hub Dust Caps, Handle Wire Bokis, Rim Washers, and a diverse array of plain washers. Our commitment to precision and customer satisfaction has driven us to not only meet industry standards but to set new benchmarks for quality and innovation. What sets us apart is our dedication to tailoring solutions we take pride in accommodating custom demands, ensuring that every customer's unique requirements are met flawlessly.
